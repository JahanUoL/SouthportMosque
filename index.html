Welcome to the website of Southport Mosque and Islamic Cultural Centre.

The 5 daily payers are held in congregation daily. For information on congregational times please refer to the Prayer (Salah) timetable

We are here to inform you about the services available at Southport Mosque as well as providing you with a means of acquiring authentic information in regards to the religion of Islam.

Our hope and aim here at Southport Mosque is to make your visit to our website worthwhile and enjoyable giving you good reason to continue to visit. The contents of our website will be determined by the wishes and needs of viewers like yourself; your comments, suggestions and queries would therefore be warmly received by our team.

I would also like to take this opportunity to express my utmost appreciation for the efforts of our whole team.
